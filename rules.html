Server Rules

Please read and follow these rules to ensure a positive experience for everyone

General Conduct

1
Respect All Players
Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or toxic behavior will be tolerated.
Permanent Ban
2
No Griefing
Do not destroy, modify, or steal from other players' builds without permission. This includes team bases.
Permanent Ban
3
Keep Chat Clean
No spam, excessive caps, or inappropriate content in chat. Keep conversations friendly and appropriate.
Warning → Mute

Gameplay Rules

4
No Cheating or Hacking
Any use of hacked clients, x-ray, or unfair advantages is strictly prohibited. Our Skyline 2.0 anti-cheat will detect and ban violators.
Permanent Ban
5
No Exploiting Bugs
Report any bugs or exploits to staff immediately. Using exploits for personal gain will result in punishment.
Temporary Ban
6
PvP Guidelines
PvP is allowed, but spawn killing and excessive targeting of new players is not permitted.
Warning → Kick

Building & Teams

7
Appropriate Builds Only
No inappropriate or offensive structures. Keep your builds creative and respectful.
Removal → Ban
8
Team Betrayal
Betraying your team by stealing, griefing, or leaking coordinates is not allowed.
Temporary Ban
9
Lag Machines
Creating machines or builds designed to lag the server is strictly forbidden.
Permanent Ban

Economy & Trading

10
No Scamming
All trades must be fair and honest. Scamming other players will result in immediate punishment.
Permanent Ban
11
Fair Trading
Use the economy system fairly. Price manipulation and monopolies that harm the server economy are not allowed.
Warning

Rules are subject to change. Staff have final say in all situations.
