About Our Server

The Skyline SMP Experience

Skyline SMP is a semi-vanilla survival multiplayer server focused on building a friendly community and amazing creations.

  • Established Community: Founded in 2025, we've grown into a thriving community of dedicated players who help each other and collaborate on amazing projects.
  • Active Staff Team: Our dedicated moderators and admins ensure a safe, friendly environment for everyone while organizing regular events and activities.
  • Regular Updates: We keep our server up-to-date with the latest Minecraft versions and continuously improve our custom features based on community feedback.
  • Community Projects: Join massive community builds, participate in themed districts, and contribute to server-wide initiatives that showcase our collective creativity.

Server Rules

To ensure everyone has a great time, please follow these simple rules. Our community thrives on respect, creativity, and fair play.

01

Respect All Players

Be kind and respectful to everyone. No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or excessive profanity. Treat others as you would like to be treated.

02

No Griefing or Stealing

Respect other players' builds and items. Don't destroy or take what isn't yours without permission. This includes pranks that cause permanent damage.

03

No Cheating or Exploits

Play fair. No hacked clients, X-ray, duplication glitches, or other unfair advantages. We regularly monitor for cheating and take it very seriously.

04

Build Responsibly

Keep a reasonable distance from other players' builds unless invited. Avoid lag-inducing redstone contraptions and excessive entity farms that impact server performance.
