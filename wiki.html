Getting Started

Welcome to Skyline 2.0! Here's everything you need to know to begin your journey.

How to Join

Connect to Skyline 2.0 using the following server address:

play.skyline2.net

Make sure you're running Minecraft Java Edition version 1.21 or higher.

First Steps

  • Read the server rules with /rules
  • Join or create a team with /team
  • Set up your home location with /sethome
  • Start gathering resources and building your base
  • Join our Discord community for updates and support

What Makes Skyline 2.0 Special?

Economy & Trading

Master the economy system and become a trading tycoon.

Currency System

Skyline 2.0 uses a custom currency called Skycoins. Earn them by:

  • Mining valuable resources
  • Completing server challenges
  • Trading with other players
  • Participating in events
  • Selling items at shops

Trading Commands

/balance - Check your Skycoin balance
/pay [player] [amount] - Send money to another player
/shop - Open the server shop
/trade [player] - Request a trade with another player

Player Shops

Create your own shop to sell items to other players:

  • Place a chest with items you want to sell
  • Use /shop create to set up pricing
  • Players can buy directly from your chest
  • Earn passive income while offline

Teams System

Join forces with other players and dominate together.

Creating a Team

Start your own team and invite friends to join:

/team create [name] - Create a new team
/team invite [player] - Invite a player to your team
/team accept - Accept a team invitation

Team Features

  • Shared Resources - Team bank and shared storage
  • Team Chat - Private communication channel
  • Team Territory - Claim land together
  • Team Ranks - Assign roles and permissions
  • Team Alliances - Form partnerships with other teams

Team Management

/team info - View team information
/team chat - Toggle team chat
/team kick [player] - Remove a player from team
/team leave - Leave your current team

Commands

Complete list of available commands on Skyline 2.0.

Teleportation (TPA)

/tpa [player] - Request to teleport to a player
/tpahere [player] - Request a player to teleport to you
/tpaccept - Accept a teleport request
/tpdeny - Deny a teleport request

Home & Warps

/sethome [name] - Set a home location
/home [name] - Teleport to your home
/delhome [name] - Delete a home location
/warp [name] - Teleport to a public warp

General Commands

/spawn - Return to spawn
/back - Return to previous location
/rules - View server rules
/help - Get help and support

Anti-Cheat System

Learn about our powerful anti-cheat technology developed by vyxzl.

Skyline 2.0 Anti-Cheat

Our custom anti-cheat system, developed by vyxzl, ensures fair gameplay for all players. It monitors and prevents:

  • Movement Cheats - Fly, speed, teleport hacks
  • Combat Cheats - Kill aura, reach, auto-clicker
  • World Cheats - X-ray, nuker, scaffold
  • Exploit Prevention - Duplication, item manipulation

How It Works

The anti-cheat runs silently in the background, analyzing player behavior in real-time:

  • Advanced pattern recognition
  • Machine learning detection algorithms
  • Automatic violation logging
  • Staff notification system
  • Regular updates to counter new cheats

False Positives

While rare, false positives can occur. If you believe you were falsely flagged:

  • Contact staff immediately via Discord
  • Provide details about what you were doing
  • Our team will review the logs
  • Legitimate players are always unbanned quickly

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions answered.

When does the server launch?

Skyline 2.0 launches on December 1st, 2025. Join our Discord for countdown updates!

What version of Minecraft do I need?

You need Minecraft Java Edition version 1.21 or higher to play on Skyline 2.0.

Is the server cracked/offline mode?

No, Skyline 2.0 is a premium server. You need a legitimate Minecraft account to join.

Can I use mods?

Quality of life mods like Optifine, minimaps, and inventory management are allowed. Cheat mods are strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.

How do I report a player?

Use /report [player] [reason] in-game or contact staff on Discord with evidence.

Is there a world border?

Yes, the world has a border to ensure optimal server performance. The border size may be expanded based on player count.

How often does the server reset?

Skyline 2.0 is designed to be a long-term world. We do not plan regular resets, but may reset for major Minecraft updates.
